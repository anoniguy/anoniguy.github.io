Who I am

Hey, I'm Mark. I make games and work with code. Most of the time I just take an idea and start building, then figure things out as I go. I enjoy experimenting with different kinds of projects, especially ones that feel a bit unusual or challenging. That includes things like game systems, small tools, or random ideas that turn into something bigger than expected. I also create content where I share what I'm working on, what I'm learning, and sometimes just things I find interesting. I spend a lot of time improving my skills, trying new approaches, and seeing how far I can push an idea. Even when something doesn't work out, it usually leads to something better later. That's pretty much it.

I also write posts here about what I'm working on, things I'm learning, and sometimes just random ideas or updates. It's a mix of progress logs, experiments, and whatever I feel like sharing at the time.
